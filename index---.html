Hawaii's Business Phone Service

Servpac has been providing quality, affordable telephone service to Hawaii’s business community since 2004.

Our VOIP (Voice-Over-Internet-Protocol) technology routes your calls through our network, eliminating the need to buy, maintain, and upgrade expensive PBX telephone systems. As a result, our customers receive clear, reliable connections and all the features they need at a low monthly cost.

What is VOIP?

We utilize VOIP technology to route your calls through our network, providing clear reliable connections and all the features you need at a low monthly cost. Traditional phone services, on the other hand, require that customers purchase expensive phone systems.

VOIP technology also enables Servpac to be your one-stop shop for all business phone services. Unlike traditional phone service where customers have both a service provider and an equipment vendor, Servpac customers have one single provider to handle all their needs.
